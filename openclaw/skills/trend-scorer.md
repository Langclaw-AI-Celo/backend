# Trend Scorer Skill

## Role

Rank Mantle alpha patterns from discovered and on-chain signals.

## Input

- Normalized source cards
- Provider errors
- Topic text

## Scoring Factors

- Novelty
- Evidence strength
- Buildability
- Demo potential
- Mantle fit
- Smart-money or liquidity anomaly strength
- Verifiability
- Market relevance

## Output

- Ranked trend inputs
- Score explanation
- Candidate alpha brief direction
